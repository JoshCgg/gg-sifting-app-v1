3 Circles Gospel Presentation
Visual gospel tool that crosses language barriers

How to Begin

Ask: "Can I share with you a picture of how God met my greatest need?" Draw as you talk.

Circle 1 — God's Perfect Design

Draw a circle. God created everything good — us, relationships, the world.

Circle 2 — Sin → Brokenness

Draw an arrow out, then a jagged broken circle. We try to escape brokenness on our own — but we can't get back.

The Cross — Jesus (Isa Al Masih / Korbani)

"If we TURN from sin and BELIEVE in Jesus... through Him we are restored to God's perfect design." — Romans 10:9

Jesus lived a perfect life, died as Korbani (sacrifice) for our sin, rose 3 days later. Draw a cross. Arrow back to God's design.

The Three Responses

  • 🟢 Green — Believed: Pray together. Immediately begin 411 discipleship.
  • 🟡 Yellow — Open: Schedule follow-up. Give Jesus film. Note their address.
  • 🔴 Red — Not ready: Offer prayer. Leave film. Keep relationship warm. Move on.

Key Bridge Phrases by Language

  • 🕌 Assalamu Alaikum — Peace be upon you (Arabic/Urdu)
  • 🇧🇩 Kemon achen — How are you? (Bengali)
  • 🙏 Namaste — Greeting (Hindi/Nepali)
  • ✝️ Isa Al Masih — Jesus the Messiah
  • 🐑 Korbani — Sacrifice (powerful bridge to the cross)

House of Peace Search — Luke 10
Finding Persons of Peace door-to-door
"Whatever house you enter, first say 'Peace be to this house!'" — Luke 10:5

Your Opening Line

"Hi, we're volunteers praying blessings over families in the neighborhood. Could we offer a prayer for your home?"

Three Signs of a Person of Peace

  • ✅ Accepts the Messenger — welcomes you warmly
  • ✅ Accepts the Message — receptive to what you share
  • ✅ Accepts the Mission — wants others in their community to know

Your Responsibility

  • Pray for laborers before you go out
  • Go and find the Person of Peace
  • When found: STAY and invest — the POP gathers their own community
  • No POP found: leave and keep searching

If Someone Is Very Open

Call your team leader immediately. Schedule Bible study. Bring a local believer if possible.

3 P's When You Enter a Home

  • ⚡ Power — Pray in the authority of Jesus
  • ☮️ Presence — Embody the peace of Christ
  • 📢 Proclamation — Speak the Good News

411 Discipleship Tool
4 questions · 1 paper · 1 hour — for new disciples
"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations..." — Matthew 28:19

The 4 Questions

  • ❓ WHY? Your identity as Ambassador (2 Cor. 5:17–21)
  • 👤 WHO? Persons of Peace + your Oikos network
  • 💬 WHAT? The 3 Circles gospel
  • 📅 WHEN? Specific calendar goals set before they leave

Oikos Map (WHO? Part 2)

Draw a circle with the person's name. Map relationships far from God — 4 generations out. This becomes their personal prayer and evangelism strategy.

4 WHEN? Goals to Set

  • Daily time to PRAY for their oikos
  • Name someone to SHARE gospel with this week
  • Schedule to TRAIN someone in 411
  • Schedule START — first Commands of Christ meeting at obeychrist.com

Prayer Walking Guide
How to intercede powerfully for your area
"The harvest is plentiful, but the laborers are few. Pray therefore to the Lord of the harvest." — Luke 10:2

What to Pray

  • Spiritual breakthrough in every home
  • Salvations — pray for people by name
  • For laborers — more workers in this harvest
  • For Persons of Peace — ask God to highlight them
  • Against spiritual darkness — that bondage be broken

Tips

  • Try to eat in the neighborhood — stay immersed
  • If asked what you're doing: "Praying blessings — can we pray for you?"
  • Note where prayer feels heavy or blocked — tell your leader
  • Take breaks — this is spiritually exhausting

Holy Spirit's Power — John 14:12
Why you can do greater things than you think
"Whoever believes in me will also do the works that I do; and greater works than these will he do." — John 14:12

The Shift: Knowledge → Power

The disciples had 3½ years with Jesus and still fled at the cross. What changed? The Holy Spirit at Pentecost. Peter went from denying Christ to boldly preaching to thousands.

You Carry That Same Spirit

You are a "little Christ" — carrying Jesus' presence into every conversation. A lack of boldness is a sign of not walking in the Spirit's power.

"Do not be drunk with wine... but be filled with the Spirit." — Ephesians 5:18

Before You Go Out — Group Prayer

Groups of 3. Lay hands on each person. Ask God to fill them so that when they speak, their words carry His authority.

Cultural & Religious Awareness
Islam basics and cultural engagement tips

What Muslims Believe

  • One God (Allah) — no partners; avoid "Son of God" language initially
  • Jesus (Isa) is honored as a prophet and the Messiah
  • Strong respect for Scripture — use "Injil" (Gospel) language
  • Five pillars: Shahada, Prayer (5×/day), Fasting, Zakat, Hajj

Bridge Points

  • Isa Al Masih — Muslims deeply respect Isa; start here
  • Korbani — Sacrifice concept connects to the cross powerfully
  • Opening: "What do you think about Isa Al Masih?"

Cultural Respect

  • Remove shoes when entering a home if they do
  • Accept offered tea or food — refusing can offend
  • Greet with Assalamu Alaikum — they will light up
  • Don't press conversation during prayer times
  • Men primarily with men; women with women
